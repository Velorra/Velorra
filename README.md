<h1 align="center">About Velorra</h1>

<p align="justify">
  <b>Velorra</b> Is A <b>Global Product And Technology Solutions Company</b> Established In <b>2025</b>, Headquartered In <b>New Delhi, India</b> With An Additional Presence In <b>Kolkata, West Bengal</b>.
</p>

<p align="justify">
  We Are Strategically Positioned At The Convergence Of Technological Advancement And Human-Centric Solutions With A Core Focus On <b>Project Management</b> And <b>Software Development</b>.
</p>

<p align="justify">
  The Name "<b>Velorra</b>" Is A Fusion Of <b>Velocity</b> And <b>Aurora</b> — Symbolizing <b>Swift Transformation</b> And <b>New Beginnings</b> In The Digital Landscape.
</p>

<br/>

> **Our Objective:** Support Organizations With Reliable Digital Solutions That Improve Collaboration, Simplify Project Tracking, And Drive Measurable Results — Serving Clients Across **100+ Countries** And **18+ Industries**.
